Sports Clubs

FWNS-MD fields competitive teams in soccer, cricket, and volleyball — representing our community across Maryland and beyond.

⚽

Soccer

Adult & Youth (U15) Teams · BANA Cup & Regional Competitions

FWNS-MD fields both adult and youth soccer teams, competing in some of the most recognized Nepali soccer competitions in the Mid-Atlantic region. Our U15 youth program has been a particular source of pride, producing back-to-back championship wins.

🏆

Himalaya Baltimore U15 Championship

Our U15 team claimed the championship title at the Himalaya Baltimore U15 Soccer Tournament, defending their position as top youth team.

🏆

BANA U15 Soccer Championship

FWNS-MD's U15 squad won the BANA U15 Soccer Tournament Championship, further cementing the program's dominance in youth athletics.

⚽

BANA Cup Participation

Our adult soccer team actively participates in the BANA Cup — one of the region's most recognized Nepali soccer competitions.

🏐

Volleyball — Far-Western Volleyball Club

Maryland Cup Organizers · Interstate Competition

In coordination with the Far-Western Volleyball Club, FWNS-MD organized the landmark FWNS Maryland Cup 2025 — a historic inter-state volleyball tournament that turned Maryland into a national platform for Nepali volleyball excellence.

FWNS Maryland Cup 2025 — Inter-State Volleyball Tournament

Held on August 9–10, 2025 in Jessup, Maryland. Attended by Maryland State Delegates and featuring teams from across the United States.

Cricket — Far-Western Cricket Club

Regional & Interstate Tournaments · Multi-Team Representation

FWNS-MD's cricket program has maintained strong regional and interstate representation, fielding multiple teams at competitive tournaments across Maryland, Virginia, and Massachusetts. The club's ability to mobilize athletes across state lines reflects the depth and commitment of our cricket community.

  • Charm City Yeti T10 Cricket Tournament — Maryland
  • Everest & Saathi Cup 2025 — Virginia (two FWNS teams representing)
  • 3rd Rup Smriti Cricket Tournament — Boston, Massachusetts
🌍

Interstate Reach

Competed across Maryland, Virginia, and Massachusetts — expanding FWNS-MD's athletic presence beyond the Mid-Atlantic region.

👥

Multi-Team Depth

Fielded two separate FWNS teams at the Everest & Saathi Cup 2025, demonstrating strong organizational depth and player pool.
