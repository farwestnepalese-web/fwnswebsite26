How to Pay

Payment via Zelle

After submitting your application, please send your membership dues via Zelle to complete your registration.

💳
Zelle
farwestnepalese@gmail.com
Please include your name and membership type in the Zelle note.

Your membership becomes active upon payment confirmation and EC approval. The General Secretary will contact you with confirmation.

Approval Process

How it works

1

Submit Application

Fill out the online membership form or in person at a FWNS-MD event.

2

Review

General Members are reviewed by the General Secretary. Life Members are reviewed by the full Executive Committee.

3

Pay Dues

Once approved, send your dues via Zelle to farwestnepalese@gmail.com.

4

Welcome!

You'll receive confirmation and be added to the official FWNS-MD membership roster.
