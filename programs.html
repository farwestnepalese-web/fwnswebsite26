Signature Program · Annual

Gaura Parva

Far-Western New Year Festival · Held Every August

Gaura Parva is the premier cultural event of FWNS-MD and a cornerstone of Far-Western identity in Maryland. One of the most cherished festivals of the Far-Western Nepali community, it celebrates the arrival of the new year, the bounty of harvest, and the enduring bonds of family and community.

Under the current executive leadership, FWNS-MD organized the 3rd and 4th Gaura Parva celebrations, each bringing together hundreds of community members across generations. The events feature traditional rituals, cultural performances, community recognition, Deuda dancing, and organized program management — reflecting both cultural authenticity and organizational maturity.

Gaura Parva has received civic recognition from Baltimore City and County governments, establishing it as a respected annual cultural institution that reinforces heritage preservation and intergenerational unity across the Nepali diaspora in Maryland.

Signature Program · Annual

Bishu Parva

Nepali New Year Celebration · Held Every Spring

Bishu Parva is FWNS-MD's celebration of the Nepali New Year — one of the most cherished traditions of the Far-Western region. The festival brings together members of the Nepali diaspora across Maryland and neighboring states for cultural performances, traditional Nepali cuisine, and family-friendly activities.

The 3rd Bishu Parva (2025) continued the preservation of Far-Western New Year traditions while expanding youth and family engagement. The event strengthened regional identity and cultural continuity, reinforcing FWNS-MD's commitment to sustaining traditional values within a structured organizational framework.

Bishu Parva has received positive engagement and recognition at the community level, further positioning FWNS-MD as a key cultural organizer in Maryland. It is particularly meaningful for second-generation community members connecting with their Far-Western heritage.

Youth & Community Initiative

Sports Development · Youth Engagement · Community Support

Beyond cultural celebrations, FWNS-MD is deeply committed to the long-term growth and well-being of our community — particularly our youth. Our Youth & Community Initiative brings together sports development, family programming, civic engagement, and community support under one coordinated effort.

On the sports side, FWNS-MD's Under-15 soccer program has produced championship-winning teams, giving young players in our community a structured and competitive environment to grow. Our affiliated sports clubs in soccer, cricket, and volleyball provide pathways for youth and adults alike to stay active, build teamwork, and represent our community with pride.

Our inaugural Kids Day Out program — a coordinated family outing to the Maryland Zoo — marked a significant step toward structured youth engagement beyond sports, emphasizing experiential learning, family bonding, and investment in the next generation of community leaders. It is the first of what we intend to be a growing suite of youth-centered programs.

Through civic meet & greet events with elected officials and community leaders, FWNS-MD also ensures that youth and families stay connected to the broader civic landscape — understanding their rights, their voice, and their place in Maryland's diverse community.

Community Programs

Annual Program Calendar

In addition to our signature festivals, FWNS-MD runs a full calendar of cultural, civic, youth, and community programs throughout the year.

Cultural · Annual

Dashain & Tihar Celebration

One of Nepal's most important festivals, celebrated community-wide with cultural programs, music, and family gatherings.

Cultural · Annual

Teej Celebration

A traditional festival honoring women, celebrated with song, dance, and community participation by FWNS-MD members.

Cultural · Annual

Deusi Bhailo

A beloved Tihar tradition where community members go door-to-door singing Deusi songs, spreading joy and raising community funds.

Cultural · Annual

Holi Celebration

The festival of colors celebrated with community members of all ages, promoting joy, unity, and cultural expression.

Cultural · Annual

Nepali New Year Celebration

Community-wide new year festivities welcoming the Nepali calendar year with cultural programs and community gatherings.

Youth · Inaugural 2025

Kids Day Out

Launched for the first time in 2025, Kids Day Out brought children and families together at the Maryland Zoo for nature-based learning and community bonding — a foundational step in FWNS-MD's youth development commitment.

Sports · 2025

Maryland Cup — Inter-State Volleyball

FWNS-MD's landmark athletic event — 14 teams from across the U.S. competed in Jessup, MD on August 9–10, 2025. Attended by Maryland State Delegates.

Civic · 2025

Meet & Greet with Elected Officials

FWNS-MD hosted community meet & greet sessions with Hon. Bir Bahadur Balayar and Hon. Padam B Shahi, strengthening civic engagement for our community.

Entertainment · 2025

DT Fest with Ravi Oad & Rekha Joshi

A major cultural entertainment event featuring renowned Far-Western artists, bringing high-energy music and cultural expression to the Maryland community.

Governance · Annual

FWNS Assessment Meeting

Annual organizational review meeting where the Executive Committee presents progress reports, reviews programs, and plans for the year ahead.
