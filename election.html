Who Can Vote

Voter Eligibility

To vote in the 2026 election you must:

  • ✓ Be a dues-paying General or Life Member in good standing
  • ✓ Have your membership active by June 13, 2026
  • ✓ Be a resident of the State of Maryland
  • ✓ Present a valid photo ID on election day

Life Members may vote in any election regardless of renewal date, provided they are in good standing.

Who Can Run

Candidacy Eligibility

To file as a candidate you must be a Life Member and:

  • ✓ Hold valid Life Membership (Individual or Couple)
  • ✓ Be nominated by 2 members (one nominating, one seconding)
  • ✓ For at-large Board positions: Life Member status is sufficient
  • ✓ For President, VP, Secretaries, Treasurer: Must have held Life Membership for at least 1 year (min. 2 yrs total involvement)

Nomination fees for this election cycle will be announced by the Election Commissioner by June 13, 2026.
