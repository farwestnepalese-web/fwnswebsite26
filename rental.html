Equipment Rental

Borrow equipment for your next event. Available to FWNS-MD members and the broader community.

Important — Read Before Requesting

All equipment availability is subject to verification by the Executive Committee. Submitting this form does not guarantee availability. You will be contacted within 2–3 business days to confirm your rental. For urgent inquiries, email us directly at farwestnepalese@gmail.com.

Payment Instructions

After your rental is confirmed by our team (within 2–3 business days), please complete payment to secure your booking.

Send payment via Zelle to farwestnepalese@gmail.com

FWNS-MD Members: $75  |  Non-Members: $150

Payment is required only after we confirm equipment availability. Submitting this form does not charge you anything.
