Why Volunteer with FWNS-MD?

The Far-Western Nepalese Society of Maryland, Inc. (FWNS-MD), a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit, is committed to connecting the Far-Western Nepalese diaspora in Maryland. Our diverse initiatives in culture, education, and health are designed to build belonging, provide support, and encourage personal growth.

We are passionately focused on preserving our rich cultural heritage and social values for new generations — ensuring they remain deeply connected with their origins.
