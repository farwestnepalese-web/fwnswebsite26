Who We Are

Built by the community,
for the community

The Far-Western Nepalese Society of Maryland traces its roots to 2016, beginning as the FarWest Nepalese Welfare Trust in Baltimore. Today, we are a fully incorporated 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving the Far-Western Nepali diaspora across Maryland.

Our Mission
To build community spirit, offer support, and promote personal development within the Far-Western Nepalese diaspora.
Our Vision
To be a beacon of unity, support, and empowerment for the Far-Western Nepalese diaspora in Maryland.

What We Do

Our Programs & Initiatives

🎭

Cultural Programs

We celebrate and preserve Far-Western Nepalese culture through festivals, arts, and cultural events — including Bishu Parva, Dashain, Tihar, and community gatherings that foster heritage and belonging.

🎓

Education & Youth

Educational workshops, scholarship support, and mentorship programs designed to enhance opportunities for both youth and adults in our community.

🏥

Health & Wellness

We organize health camps, wellness programs, and awareness campaigns focused on the physical and mental well-being of our community members.

⚽

Sports & Recreation

FWNS-MD supports affiliated sports clubs in football, cricket, and volleyball. We host tournaments that promote teamwork, fitness, and friendly community competition.

🤝

Community Support

Our support services assist members in need — providing financial aid during illness, death, or disaster — and maintaining a strong safety net for every member.

🗳️

Civic Engagement

We foster relationships with local, state, and federal government, advocating for the rights and representation of Nepali Americans in Maryland.
